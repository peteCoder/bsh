Save

Earn up to 3.55% (AER/Gross fixed) interest on your savings. That’s on a 12-month Fixed Pot with a £500 minimum deposit to open. (Fixed means the money will be locked away for 12 months while you earn interest on it.) We offer Easy Access Savings too, which let you get your money the next working day!

Spend

Get instant notifications the second you pay. Set budgets for things like groceries and going out, and get warnings if you’re spending too fast (if you want them).

Manage

Pay Direct Debits through Diamond Horizon and we’ll tell you if they’re higher for the upcoming month. So no nasty surprises.

Using Diamond Horizon Abroad

Use Diamond Horizon anywhere in the world that accepts Mastercard. We don’t charge any fees for spending abroad, and we don’t mark up the exchange rate – unlike most other banks. Travelling with Diamond Horizon

A better way to pay later

Use Diamond Horizon Flex to spread the cost of pretty much anything. If eligible it’s interest free over 3 instalments. Or there’s 6 or 12 instalments at 24% APR representative (variable) if you want more time.

Diamond Flex is credit for eligible 18+ year olds. Ts&Cs apply. Carefully consider the cost of using credit for purchases. Missed payments may negatively impact your credit score and you may lose the interest-free rate on existing plans. Representative example: 24% APR representative (variable). £1200 credit limit. 24% yearly interest (variable).

Borrowing: Loans and Overdrafts

We offer overdrafts up to £2,000 and loans up to £25,000. Checking if you're eligible won't leave a mark on your credit score, there are no lengthy forms to fill in, and no charges for paying us back early.

Our representative APR is 10.5% for loans more than £10,000, up to £25,000. For loans up to £10,000 it’s 23.9%. How does our overdraft compare? Our representative APR is 39.0%. You can use the APR to compare the cost of different credit products.
